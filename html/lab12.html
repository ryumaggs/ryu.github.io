The Dataset

The provided dataset is a Comma Separated Value file from GapMinder.

We will use this dataset to try to predict the category of income a country will have given the income and life expectancy in 2006 and the change from 1996's income and life expectancy. We also have information about the region in which a country belongs.

Finally we want to see which attributes and combination of attributes play a significant role in deciding the output, by using association learning and analysing the resulting associations.
